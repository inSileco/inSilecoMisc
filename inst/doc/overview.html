inSilecoMisc, an overview

inSileco group

2019-01-30

This vignette is an overview of inSilecoMisc organized around five major themes:

  1. strings manipulations
  2. vector manipulations
  3. data frames manipulations
  4. Bibliography operations
  5. Mathematical functions
